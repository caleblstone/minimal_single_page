Decomputation
A Podcast On Creative Tools
Decomputation is a show dedicated to the community building and using new digital tools of creation. We'll interview artists building sandcastle-building software and folks hosting algo-raves in Minecraft. We're talking to programmers building viruses as art, and artists building software to mine cryptocurrency to bail folks out of jail.
